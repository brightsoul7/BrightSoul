Gardening!!

Gardening: Spending time in a garden or tending to plants allows you to connect with nature. The sights, smells, and sounds of the garden can have a calming effect, bringing you closer to the natural world, which is known to reduce stress.Gardening encourages you to be present in the moment, focusing on the task at hand. This mindful engagement can help reduce anxiety by diverting your attention from racing thoughts and worries.
